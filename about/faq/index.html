Frequently-asked Questions

Festival 101

When does festival take place?
The Inman Park Spring Festival always take place on the last weekend of April. This year it runs from April 28 - April 29.
What are the hours?
On Friday, the Tour of Homes runs from Noon until 4:00 p.m (there are no other Festival events on Friday.) On Saturday and Sunday the Tour of Homes runs from Noon until 6:00 p.m. On Saturday, Festival (excluding the Tour of Homes) begins at 11:00 a.m. and runs until 8:30 p.m. On Sunday, the times are 11:00 until 7:00 p.m.
When is the parade?
The parade occurs on Saturday at 2:00 p.m.
Are Pets Allowed?
Please remember that, by City of Atlanta Ordnance, pets are not allowed at Festivals, "unless part of a planned festival program or assisting persons with disabilities". This prohibition includes "dogs, cats, snakes, birds, pigs, etc.", and applies whether or not the pet is leashed. As the ordnance says, this is for the pet's protection as well as for the protection of festival patrons. Thanks!!

Tour of Homes Tickets

How much do Tour of Homes Tickets Cost?
Tour of Homes tickets are $20 in advance and $25 starting Friday, April 27.
Are there group ticket rates?
Yes, group discounts are available for groups of 10 or more.

Food

What food is available at the festival?
Pizza, barbecue, gyros, sandwiches, and, of course, funnel cakes and other street festival staples. Local restaurants will also be open for business.

Sponsors

What levels of sponsorships does the festival offer?
There are several levels of sponsorships available to businesses of all sizes, including Gold and Silver levels as well as Friends of Festival designations.
How do I learn more about sponsorships?
