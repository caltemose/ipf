Welcome to Atlanta's Favorite Street Festival

During the last full weekend in April each year, Inman Park proudly presents a neighborhood festival widely regarded as Atlanta’s most spirited and eclectic.

Please join us for the 47th Inman Park Spring Festival and Tour Of Homes.

The 2018 Inman Park Festival takes place on Saturday April 28 & Sunday April 29 with a Tour of Homes preview on Friday April 27.
