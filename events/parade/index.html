Inman Park Festival Parade

Come see our Butterfly, Trash Monarch, Precision Attache Case Drill Team, and the Seed & Feed Marching Abominables take to the street in the city's funkiest and most eclectic spring parade. This favorite of Festival begins at Edgewood and Euclid on Saturday April 28 at 2:00 PM (rain or shine) and runs to Austin Avenue at the foot of Little Five Points.

The AJC considers the parade one of 10 Things Every Atlanta Resident Should Do:

Take in a parade with personality. (Intown Atlanta) Inman Park Spring Festival's parade is a people watching paradise. Colorful costumes, creative themes, and the Seed & Feed Marching Abominable invite spring with sass and funk.

Download the Parade Application if you'd like to register to march in the parade.
