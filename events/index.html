Festival Events

Tour of HomesParadeMusicDanceKidzone

The Inman Park Spring Festival includes many different events to suit all interests:

  • a tour of historic and beautiful neighborhood homes
  • the best festival parade in town
  • three stages of music all weekend
  • dance performances by local professional dance groups
  • a kidzone space for kids to play

No matter what event or activity brings you to festival, plan to spend the day or weekend in the neighborhood as there is much to see and do.
