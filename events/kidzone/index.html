Fun Kidzone photos go here

Festival provides a dedicated space for kids to play that includes slides, obstacles courses, crafts, a great playground, riding toys for our youngest guests and the CHANCE TO JOIN A CIRCUS, all of them FREE!

The Kidzone is located in Springvale Park, off Elizabeth Street and Waverly Way, behind the main music tent. It is a great place to let the kids play while parents or chaperones enjoy one of Atlanta's oldest urban park spaces. Kidzone hours are from Noon until 6 PM on Saturday April 30 and Sunday May 1.
